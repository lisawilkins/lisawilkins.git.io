Persuading pixels to achieve their maximum potential Résumé

I am a Senior UX Designer building apps on OTT and mobile at NBC News Digital in Seattle. My passions are user-testing, reading analytics, agile development, being a liaison between groups to build close-knit teams who make beautiful products together. I collaborate with PMs, developers, designers, researchers, and stakeholders who are eager to learn and evolve. My most successful team experiences have been those who value radical candor and productive criticism based on (but not limited to) data and research. I base my success on interfaces that are effortless to navigate, meeting the goals of business and users alike.
